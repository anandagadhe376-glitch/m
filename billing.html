Siplora Restaurant POS — Billing & Service Niyam
01 · PAYMENT
Bill ka payment — Cash, Card, ya UPI ke through hona chahiye. Payment counter par hi accept ki jaayegi. Bill dene ke baad koi changes nahi honge.
02 · GST & TAXES
Sabhi bills par applicable GST (CGST + SGST) lagegi jo government norms ke anusar hogi. GST bill mein clearly mention ki jaayegi. GST refundable nahi hai.
03 · DISCOUNT & OFFERS
Coupon codes aur discounts sirf valid period mein applicable honge. Ek bill par ek hi coupon apply hoga. Management ka decision final hoga. Misuse pe coupon cancel ho sakta hai.
04 · FOOD & QUALITY
Khaana serve hone ke baad quality complaint 10 minute ke andar karni hogi. Serve ki gayi dish wapis nahi li jaayegi. Allergy se related information order ke time batayein.
05 · TABLE & RESERVATION
Table reservation confirm karne ke liye advance deposit required hai. 30 minute se zyada late aane par reservation cancel ho sakti hai. Table transfer restaurant policy ke anusar hoga.
06 · REFUND POLICY
Prepaid orders ka refund sirf restaurant ki galti hone par diya jaayega. Refund maximum 5-7 working days mein process hoga. Partial refund ka final decision manager ka hoga.
07 · DELIVERY ORDERS
Delivery charge alag se applicable hoga. Delivery time traffic aur weather se affect ho sakta hai. Order dene ke baad cancellation sirf 5 minute ke andar accept hogi.
08 · PRIVACY & DATA
Customer ka naam, phone aur billing data securely store kiya jaata hai. Ye data sirf restaurant operations ke liye use hoga. Kisi third party ko share nahi kiya jaayega bina permission ke.
09 · CONDUCT & BEHAVIOUR
Restaurant mein proper conduct zaroori hai. Staff ke saath disrespectful behaviour par entry refuse ki ja sakti hai. Premises mein smoking/alcohol bina permission not allowed hai.
10 · DISPUTE RESOLUTION
For any billing dispute, please contact the restaurant manager: 9021758399. Dispute resolve na ho toh Pune Consumer Forum mein complaint ki ja sakti hai. All disputes subject to Pune jurisdiction.

Terms & Conditions
Siplora Restaurant POS — Sabhi Niyam aur Shart
01 · PAYMENT
Bill ka payment — Cash, Card, ya UPI ke through hona chahiye. Payment counter par hi accept ki jaayegi. Bill dene ke baad koi changes nahi honge.
02 · GST & TAXES
Sabhi bills par applicable GST (CGST + SGST) lagegi jo government norms ke anusar hogi. GST bill mein clearly mention ki jaayegi. GST refundable nahi hai.
03 · DISCOUNT & OFFERS
Coupon codes aur discounts sirf valid period mein applicable honge. Ek bill par ek hi coupon apply hoga. Misuse pe coupon cancel ho sakta hai.
04 · FOOD & QUALITY
Khaana serve hone ke baad quality complaint 10 minute ke andar karni hogi. Serve ki gayi dish wapis nahi li jaayegi. Allergy se related info order ke time batayein.
05 · TABLE & RESERVATION
Table reservation ke liye advance deposit required hai. 30 minute late aane par reservation cancel ho sakti hai. Table transfer restaurant policy ke anusar hoga.
06 · REFUND POLICY
Prepaid orders ka refund sirf restaurant ki galti hone par diya jaayega. Refund 5–7 working days mein process hoga. Partial refund ka decision manager ka hoga.
07 · DELIVERY ORDERS
Delivery charge alag se applicable hoga. Delivery time traffic aur weather se affect ho sakta hai. Order dene ke baad cancellation sirf 5 minute ke andar accept hogi.
08 · PRIVACY & DATA
Customer ka naam, phone aur billing data securely store kiya jaata hai. Ye data sirf restaurant operations ke liye use hoga. Kisi third party ko share nahi kiya jaayega.
09 · CONDUCT & BEHAVIOUR
Restaurant mein proper conduct zaroori hai. Staff ke saath disrespectful behaviour par entry refuse ki ja sakti hai. Premises mein smoking bina permission allowed nahi.
10 · DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Billing dispute ke liye manager se contact karein: 9021758399. Dispute resolve na ho toh Pune Consumer Forum mein complaint ki ja sakti hai.

SIPLORA POS — HOW TO USE
DASHBOARD
Dashboard par aaj ka revenue, live orders, staff count, tables, aur pending tasks dikhte hain. Notification bell se real-time alerts milte hain.
BILLING
Auto Bill: Table select karo → order auto load hoga → Print/WhatsApp karo.
Manual Bill: Customer details + items fill karo → discount/coupon lagao → Print/Download.
Digital Bill: Phone number daalo → WhatsApp pe directly bill bhejo.
️ TABLE MANAGEMENT
Table par click karo QR code dekhne ke liye. Long press se status change karo (Available / Occupied / Reserved). + Add Table se nayi table add karo.
MENU & STOCK
Menu mein dishes add/edit/delete karo. Categories customize karo. Stock section mein inventory track karo — low stock alert automatically aayega.
SIPLORA AI
Top mein "Siplora AI" button dabao ya mic icon se voice command do. Apni restaurant ke baare mein kuch bhi poochho — revenue, stock, tips, reports sab milega!
STAFF & CUSTOMERS
Staff section mein attendance aur performance track karo. Customer section mein loyal customers manage karo, birthdays dekho, aur coupon bhejo.
SUPPORT
Koi bhi problem aaye toh Customer Care call karo: 9021758399. Sidebar mein hamesha number dikhta hai.
